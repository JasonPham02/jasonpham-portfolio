The Role of Cryptocurrency in Financial Privacy and Cybersecurity

In my research paper, "The Role of Cryptocurrency in Financial Privacy and Cybersecurity," I conduct an in-depth analysis of the dual nature of blockchain technology. The paper moves beyond a general overview to investigate specific security vulnerabilities, such as emerging "pig butchering" scams, and examines the legal landscape through established criminological frameworks. In this paper, I investigate the critical trade-offs inherent in our increasingly digital society. It addresses key questions about the future of human connection, productivity-driven health challenges, emerging cybersecurity threats, and the large-scale impact of automation on the job market.
